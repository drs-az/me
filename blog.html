How AI Is Making My Life Better — And How It Can Improve Yours Too

We’ve all heard the promise: AI will change everything. But what does that look like in real life? For me, it meant building a suite of AI-powered tools—not to replace human connection or creativity, but to amplify it.

Over the past year, I’ve been developing a series of intelligent assistants, apps, and frameworks that reflect a simple but powerful question:

What if technology actually made us better humans?

Here’s what that looks like in action.

🛒 Grocery Genius
A smarter grocery planner, built for real life.
Grocery Genius creates efficient, personalized grocery lists based on your household size, food preferences, pantry staples, and favorite store. It even finds sale items when available.

🔡 ConnectWord
A fun, distraction-free word game.
ConnectWord is my clean, ad-free version of the commercial game we all know. It’s simple, fast, and great for sharpening the mind without cluttering it.

Livie helps you develop emotional awareness, track habits, and grow as a leader—in your relationships, your parenting, and your personal life. It’s more than journaling. It’s self-coaching powered by insight.

StorySmith helps you write heartfelt short stories—even if you’ve never written before. You bring the ideas; it brings the craft.

🧠 So… Why Build All This?

I didn’t set out to build a tech stack. I set out to build a better life—for myself, my family, and others like me.

Each of these tools was born from a real need:

  • Making healthier food choices without overthinking
  • Creating structure for emotional self-reflection
  • Supporting grief, storytelling, parenting, or connection
  • Reducing clutter in our digital lives
  • Helping people feel seen, understood, and uplifted

What ties them all together is this: AI isn’t just for efficiency—it’s for empathy.

👣 What’s Next?

We’re just getting started. I believe the future belongs to creators who use AI with purpose—not to distract or dominate, but to serve.

If you’re building something that matters—or want to—let’s connect.

Because the future is human.
And AI can help.
