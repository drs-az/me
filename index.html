CoupleSync

CoupleSync is an AI-powered relationship optimization tool that helps couples deeply understand themselves and each other. Through a comprehensive questionnaire, it creates detailed psychological profiles for each partner and analyzes the dynamic between them. These insights are then integrated into a personalized AI coach, available 24/7 to guide communication, resolve conflict, and strengthen connection. It’s like having a relationship expert in your pocket—tailored to you. Try it here.

Livie

Livie is an AI-powered coach designed to guide you through daily check-ins, micro-lessons, mood tracking, and tiny actionable steps—so you can build self-awareness, healthy routines, and a positive self-image without overwhelm. Try it here.

Story Quilt

Story Quilt is a guided storytelling experience that helps grandparents share their life stories using familiar tools like voice-to-text in Google Messages or any AI chat app. With a single prompt, users are gently led through meaningful questions, one memory at a time. They can skip questions, speak naturally, and receive a beautifully written story crafted from their own words. No accounts, no apps—just their voice, their story, and their legacy. Try it here.

HexaShift

HexaShift is a lightweight, offline encryption tool built for privacy, simplicity, and control. Designed as a standalone Progressive Web App (PWA), it enables peer-to-peer message and file encryption using high-entropy passphrases — with no servers, no accounts, and no data collection. Once loaded, HexaShift works entirely offline. Try it here.
